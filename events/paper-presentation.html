PAPER PRESENTATION

Rules

The event is based on presenting a paper about a particular topic that can be chosen by the participants according to their choice.

Participants can participate in solo or group(maximum 3)

The topic can be selected by the participants according to their choice.

Presentation must be in PPT form.

Time limit of the presentation will be 5-6 minutes.

Each team will have a questioning session for 2-3 minutes.

Eligibility

All UG and PG students can participate.
